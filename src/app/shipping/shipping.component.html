What is fashionIndia's Shipping Policy?

We strive to deliver products purchased from fashionIndia in excellent condition and in the fastest time possible. If this is your first order with fashionIndia, shipping will be completely FREE. Also, for all the subsequent purchases of Rs. 1299* or more, we will deliver the order to your doorstep free of cost. A shipping charge of Rs. 149 will be applicable to all orders under Rs. 1299.

If the order is cancelled, lost or un-delivered to your preferred location, we will refund the complete order amount including any shipping charges, if paid online.

If you return an order delivered to you, order shipping charges will not be refunded. However if you self-ship your returns, we will reimburse self-shipment charges based on fashionIndia's Returns Policy. For accounts whose return behavior violate our fair usage policy, a delivery charge of Rs. 149 will be levied on all orders, irrespective of order value.



*Order value is calculated after applying discounts/VAT/GST or any other applicable charges.
